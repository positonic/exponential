## Overview

The Weekly Review is your opportunity to step back, reflect on the past week, and set up the next week for success. This ritual ensures you stay aligned with your goals and maintain momentum.

### Key Capabilities

- **Week reflection**: Review accomplishments and challenges
- **Team sharing**: Share reviews with team members
- **Slack integration**: Send summaries to team channels
- **Outcome planning**: Set weekly outcomes
- **Progress tracking**: Monitor goal advancement

## Weekly Review Structure

### Review Past Week

Reflect on what happened:

1. **Accomplishments**: What did you complete?
2. **Challenges**: What obstacles arose?
3. **Learnings**: What insights did you gain?
4. **Energy**: How did energy levels fluctuate?

### Plan Next Week

Set up for success:

1. **Outcomes**: Define measurable results for the week
2. **Priorities**: Identify most important actions
3. **Projects**: Review project progress
4. **Calendar**: Check upcoming commitments

## Conducting Your Weekly Review

### Recommended Timing

- **When**: Friday afternoon or Sunday evening
- **Duration**: 30-60 minutes
- **Location**: Quiet, distraction-free space
- **Frequency**: Every week, same time

### Step-by-Step Process

**Step 1: Clear Your Inbox**
- Process all inbox items
- Assign to projects or delete
- Start fresh for new week

**Step 2: Review Past Week**
- Check completed outcomes
- Review accomplishments
- Note challenges faced
- Capture key learnings

**Step 3: Review Active Projects**
- Check project status
- Update progress percentages
- Identify blocked items
- Adjust priorities

**Step 4: Review Goals**
- Check alignment of current work
- Update goal progress
- Identify goals needing attention
- Celebrate goal achievements

**Step 5: Set Weekly Outcomes**
- Define 3-5 key outcomes
- Make them measurable
- Link to goals
- Assign to projects

**Step 6: Plan Key Actions**
- Identify critical tasks
- Set realistic due dates
- Prioritize appropriately
- Schedule time blocks

## Team Sharing

Share your weekly review with team members for alignment and accountability.

### Enable Sharing

1. Go to **Weekly Review** settings
2. Click **Sharing Settings**
3. Select teams to share with
4. Enable sharing

### View Team Reviews

When sharing is enabled:
1. Navigate to **Weekly Review**
2. Click **Team Reviews**
3. View summaries from team members
4. Add comments or reactions

### Sharing Benefits

- **Visibility**: Know what teammates are focused on
- **Alignment**: Coordinate on shared goals
- **Accountability**: Commitment to stated outcomes
- **Support**: Offer help with challenges

## Slack Integration

Send your weekly review summary to Slack channels.

### Setup

1. Ensure [Slack integration](/docs/features/slack) is configured
2. Go to **Weekly Review**
3. Click **Send to Slack**
4. Select target channel
5. Confirm send

### Slack Summary Contents

- Week's accomplishments
- Key challenges faced
- Next week's outcomes
- Help needed (if any)

### Channel Selection

Choose from available channels:
- Project channels
- Team channels
- Personal DM to self

## Weekly Outcomes

Set measurable outcomes for the week during your review.

### Creating Weekly Outcomes

1. In Weekly Review, go to outcomes section
2. Click **Add Outcome**
3. Write specific, measurable result
4. Link to supporting goal
5. Save outcome

### Outcome Status Tracking

Track progress throughout the week:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Not Started | Haven't begun |
| In Progress | Currently working on |
| Completed | Successfully finished |
| Blocked | Waiting on something |

### Weekly vs Daily Outcomes

- **Weekly**: Larger results achievable in a week
- **Daily**: Smaller daily focus items

Set weekly outcomes during review, daily outcomes each morning.

## Best Practices

### Consistency

- Same time each week
- Complete all sections
- Don't skip weeks
- Protect review time

### Reflection Quality

- Be honest about challenges
- Celebrate all wins (big and small)
- Look for patterns
- Note what worked

### Planning Realism

- Don't overcommit
- Leave buffer time
- Account for recurring tasks
- Be conservative on estimates

### Goal Alignment

- Connect weekly outcomes to goals
- Review goal progress monthly
- Adjust approach if not progressing
- Celebrate goal milestones

### Team Sharing

- Share openly and honestly
- Offer help to teammates
- Learn from others' approaches
- Build trust through vulnerability
