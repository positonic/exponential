## Overview

Routines are the bookends of a productive day. The Startup Routine prepares you for success each morning, while the Wind-Down Routine helps you reflect, recover, and set up tomorrow.

### Key Capabilities

- **Morning structure**: Consistent daily startup
- **Evening reflection**: Process the day
- **Journaling integration**: Built-in reflection sections
- **Outcome setting**: Plan daily results
- **Configurable sections**: Customize your routine

## Startup Routine

*"Win the morning, win the day."*

The Startup Routine is your morning ritual for setting intentions and preparing for a productive day.

### Location

Navigate to **Startup Routine** from the sidebar.

### Routine Sections

#### Mindset

Prepare your mental state:
- Affirmations
- Mindset reminders
- Mental preparation notes

**Configure**: Toggle `doMindset` in settings

#### Considerations

What to keep in mind today:
- Important meetings
- Key deadlines
- People to contact
- Things to remember

**Configure**: Toggle `doConsider` in settings

#### Not-To-Do List

Things to avoid today:
- Distractions to eliminate
- Bad habits to resist
- Time wasters to skip

**Configure**: Toggle `doNotToDo` in settings

#### Questions

Reflective questions to answer:
- What's most important today?
- What would make today great?
- What am I grateful for?

**Configure**: Toggle `doQuestions` in settings

#### Daily Outcomes

Set measurable results for the day:
- 3-5 key outcomes
- Specific and achievable
- Linked to weekly outcomes

Use `Cmd+Enter` to quickly add multiple outcomes.

### Morning Workflow

**Recommended time**: 15-30 minutes after waking

1. **Open Startup Routine**
2. **Review yesterday** (if not done in wind-down)
3. **Set mindset** for the day
4. **Add considerations** for awareness
5. **List not-to-dos** for focus
6. **Answer questions** for clarity
7. **Set daily outcomes** (3-5 items)
8. **Review and commit**

## Wind-Down Routine

*"End your day with reflection. Your evening routine shapes tomorrow's success."*

The Wind-Down Routine helps you process the day, capture learnings, and prepare for restful recovery.

### Location

Navigate to **Wind-Down** from the sidebar.

### Routine Sections

#### Journal

Free-form reflection with rich text:
- Process thoughts and feelings
- Document the day's events
- Write without structure
- Use formatting for clarity

#### Gratitude

Practice appreciation:
- What are you grateful for today?
- People, experiences, things
- Big and small gratitudes
- Cultivate positive mindset

#### Outcome Review

Review daily outcomes:
- What did you complete?
- What remains unfinished?
- Why or why not?
- Adjust for tomorrow

#### Exercise

Document physical activity:
- What exercise did you do?
- Duration and intensity
- How did it feel?
- Tomorrow's plan

#### Not-To-Do Review

Review avoidance list:
- Did you successfully avoid distractions?
- What tempted you?
- Adjust for tomorrow

### Evening Workflow

**Recommended time**: 15-30 minutes before sleep

1. **Open Wind-Down Routine**
2. **Complete journal** entry
3. **Write gratitudes** (3 items minimum)
4. **Review outcomes** completion
5. **Log exercise** if applicable
6. **Review not-to-do** success
7. **Save all progress**

## Configuring Routines

### Startup Configuration

Customize which sections appear:

1. Go to **Startup Routine**
2. Click **Configuration** or settings icon
3. Toggle sections on/off:
   - `doMindset` - Mental preparation
   - `doConsider` - Daily considerations
   - `doNotToDo` - Avoidance list
   - `doQuestions` - Reflective questions
4. Configuration saves automatically

### Section Persistence

Your configuration persists across sessions:
- Settings saved per user
- Applied automatically on load
- Editable anytime

## Best Practices

### Startup Routine

**Timing**:
- Same time daily (within 30 min)
- Before checking email/messages
- After morning essentials (coffee, etc.)

**Duration**:
- 15-30 minutes
- Don't rush
- Protect this time

**Focus**:
- Start with mindset
- Limit outcomes to 3-5
- Be realistic about the day

### Wind-Down Routine

**Timing**:
- Same time each evening
- Before screens/entertainment
- Allow transition time before sleep

**Duration**:
- 15-30 minutes
- Don't skip sections
- Complete the full routine

**Focus**:
- Process the day completely
- Practice genuine gratitude
- Set up tomorrow mentally

### Consistency

- Never skip routines
- Brief routine > no routine
- Track completion streak
- Adjust timing as needed

### Customization

- Enable only sections you'll use
- Add sections as habits form
- Remove sections that don't serve you
- Evolve routine over time

### Environment

- Quiet, distraction-free space
- Same location when possible
- Remove phone notifications
- Create ritual around routine
