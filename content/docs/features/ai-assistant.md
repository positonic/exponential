## Overview

The AI Assistant is your intelligent productivity partner. Chat with AI agents to get help with task management, project planning, meeting insights, and general productivity questions.

## Accessing the AI Assistant

Navigate to **Agent** in the sidebar to open the full chat interface.

## Chat Interface

The AI Assistant page has two main areas:

### Sidebar
- **New Chat** - Start a fresh conversation
- **Conversation History** - View and resume past chats
- **Agent Selection** - Choose which AI agent to talk to

### Chat Area
- **Agent Header** - Shows current active agent with dropdown to switch
- **Message History** - Your conversation with the AI
- **Input Field** - Type your questions and requests

## Using the Assistant

### Starting a Conversation

1. Navigate to **Agent** in the sidebar
2. Type your question or request in the input field
3. Press Enter or click Send
4. The AI will respond with helpful information

### Example Conversations

**Task Help:**
```
"What should I focus on today?"
"Help me prioritize my tasks for this week"
"Create a task to review the quarterly report by Friday"
```

**Project Management:**
```
"What's the status of my active projects?"
"Help me plan the marketing campaign project"
"What tasks are overdue?"
```

**Meeting Insights:**
```
"Summarize my recent meeting with the design team"
"What action items came from yesterday's standup?"
"Find all tasks related to the product launch"
```

**General Productivity:**
```
"How can I be more productive this week?"
"Help me set up a morning routine"
"What are best practices for goal setting?"
```

## Working with Agents

### Available Agents

The assistant may include multiple specialized agents. The default agent is **Paddy**, your general productivity assistant.

### Switching Agents

1. Click the agent name in the header
2. Select a different agent from the dropdown
3. Continue your conversation with the new agent

Or type `@agentname` at the start of your message to direct it to a specific agent.

### Agent Capabilities

Each agent has access to:
- Your projects and tasks
- Your goals and outcomes
- Meeting transcripts and notes
- Your knowledge base
- Integration data (Fireflies, Slack, etc.)

## Conversation Management

### Starting a New Chat

Click **New Chat** in the sidebar to start fresh. Your previous conversation is saved and can be resumed later.

### Resuming Conversations

1. Look in the sidebar for your conversation history
2. Click on any past conversation to resume it
3. The full context is restored

### Conversation Context

The AI remembers:
- The current conversation thread
- Your preferences from past interactions
- Your projects, tasks, and goals
- Recent activity in your workspace

## Tips for Better Results

### Be Specific

**Good:** "What tasks in the Marketing project are due this week?"
**Less Good:** "What's going on?"

### Provide Context

**Good:** "I'm preparing for the quarterly review meeting tomorrow. What should I focus on?"
**Less Good:** "Help me prepare."

### Ask Follow-ups

The AI remembers your conversation, so you can ask follow-up questions:
1. "What are my active projects?"
2. "Tell me more about the second one."
3. "What tasks are overdue in that project?"

### Use Natural Language

You don't need special commands. Just talk naturally:
- "Can you help me..."
- "I need to..."
- "What's the best way to..."

## Privacy & Data

### What the AI Can Access

The AI can access data in your account:
- Projects and tasks you've created
- Goals and outcomes you've defined
- Meeting transcripts (from Fireflies integration)
- Knowledge base content
- Your conversation history

### What the AI Cannot Access

- Other users' private data
- Data from workspaces you're not a member of
- External systems without configured integrations

### Conversation Storage

Conversations are stored to:
- Allow you to resume later
- Improve AI responses over time
- Provide context for follow-up questions

## Troubleshooting

### AI Not Responding

If the AI doesn't respond:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Try refreshing the page
3. Start a new conversation
4. If issues persist, contact support

### Unexpected Responses

If responses seem off:
1. Rephrase your question more specifically
2. Provide additional context
3. Start a new conversation for a fresh context

### Missing Data

If the AI doesn't know about something:
1. Ensure the data exists in Exponential
2. Check that you have the right workspace selected
3. Verify integrations are connected
